## Linux本地编译打包

```shell
./Engine/Build/BatchFiles/RunUAT.sh BuildCookRun -project=XX.uproject -build -cook -pak -stage -archive -archivedirectory=./Archive/ -package -serverconfig=Development -server -noclient -targetplatform=Linux -prereqs -nop4 -utf8output

```

不要带上选项-nocompileeditor

## 交叉打包

```shell
..\UnrealEngine\Engine\Build\BatchFiles\RunUAT.bat BuildCookRun -project="XX.uproject" -noP4 -clientconfig=%BUILD_CONFIG% -serverconfig=%BUILD_CONFIG% -compileeditor -utf8output -server -serverplatform=Linux -noclient -build -cook -allmaps -additionalcookeroptions=-CookAll -iterativecooking -compressed -stage -package -stagingdirectory=%BUILD_DIR% -cmdline=" -Messaging" -compile -archive -archivedirectory=%BUILD_DIR%

```
